    About Me

    I'm Trevor Burningham, I reside in the wonderfull mountainous state of Utah. Some of my hobbies include hiking, running, furthering my education and spending time with my sweet wife. I currently work as a Application Support Specialist at Journal Technologies Inc. I'm a certified Full Stack Web Developer, and aspiring master programmer.

    I've worked on various team projects where I've built fully function web applications that not only meet the needs of customers and the demands of the marketplace, but as well as fun, entertaining projects for leisure.

    My strengths lie in project management, and working in the back-end of projects. In the majority of the group projects I've worked on, I worked primarily on back-end, and focused greatly on bringing the front-end and back-end together.
